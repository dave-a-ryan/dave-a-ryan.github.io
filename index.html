This is the "Secret Santa" website I built for my extended family. Each year we had an issue where people had bought the same gift for the same person, and there would be returns. We also had to send our Christmas list to everyone in the family individually. With these problems in mind, I made a website to solve them. Everyone's list is up on the site for everyone to view, so there's no miscommunication. Then when you decide to make a purchase, everyone (but the person you're buying for) will see that someone bought that gift.

This was my capstone project for my coding bootcamp. One of my hobbies is playing the trading card game Magic: The Gathering. Within "Magic", there are a large number of ways to play. Depending on which way you play, you might not be equipped to play against someone else who also plays Magic but in a different "format". I built this site as a demonstration of a social Facebook-style site for users to display which formats they play, and find like-minded players in their area. You can search by zip code distance, add friends, send each other messages, and customize your profile with your favorite card.
